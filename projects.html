Our Projects

A showcase of our current and past game development work. Each project represents our commitment to innovation, atmosphere, and player experience.

Squeel

A roguelike survival horror game where a pig must escape from a procedurally generated slaughterhouse. Features permadeath, dynamic AI, and intense chase sequences.

Tech: Unity, C#, NavMesh AI, Procedural Generation

Echelon

Fast-paced arcade FPS with vertical progression. Battle through a mysterious skyscraper headquarters, uncovering a conspiracy one floor at a time.

Tech: Godot, GDScript, Custom AI, Retro Shader Pipeline

The Perfect Crime

Forensic simulation game focused on planning and cover-up mechanics. Evade modern police investigation through careful planning and execution.

Tech: Unreal Engine, Blueprint, Physics Simulation

Cryptid Tracker DEMO

Point-and-click adventure meets survival horror. Investigate mysterious disappearances in the Pacific Northwest using photography and stealth.

Tech: Unity, C#, Dialogue System, Photo Mechanics

Rhythm of Ruins DEMO

Turn-based RPG with rhythm game combat. Explore ancient dungeons where every attack requires perfect timing to your opponent's musical patterns.

Tech: Godot, Audio Engine Integration, Custom Battle System

Last Transmission DEMO

Narrative-driven space horror. Monitor security feeds and make critical decisions as the sole survivor on a derelict space station.

Tech: Unity, C#, Branching Narrative, FMV Integration

Ghost Protocol DEMO

Stealth action game where you play as a corporate spy navigating office buildings at night. Avoid security, hack terminals, extract data.

Tech: Unreal Engine, AI Perception System, Stealth Mechanics

Neon Nights DEMO

Retro racing game with a cyberpunk aesthetic. Illegal street races through procedurally lit city environments with synthwave soundtrack.

Tech: Unity, Vehicle Physics, Shader Graph, Audio Reactive

The Archivist DEMO

Walking simulator meets puzzle game. Catalog mysterious artifacts in a supernatural museum, uncovering their stories and connections.

Tech: Godot, Object Interaction System, Lore Database
