Who are we?

Do you remember the hum of the disc drive? The weight of the controller? The way simple polygons could build worlds that felt more real than reality?

That memory is our blueprint.

Fidelio Digital was forged in the echo of the 32-bit revolution. We are not a studio that imitates the past; we are channeling its untamed spirit. Our purpose is to create games that defy categorization, blending genres to forge experiences that feel like forgotten gems you’ve discovered on a dusty shelf.

Our name, Fidelio, speaks to our loyalty—a faithfulness to the daring creativity and raw atmosphere of that golden age. In our worlds, survival horror mechanics may bleed into a heartfelt narrative, and a turn-based RPG could suddenly demand rhythmic precision.

We're crafting the games that should have existed. Boot up. The memory card has space.
